Tesla aims to accelerate the world’s transition to sustainable energy. Founded in 2003, it focuses on electric vehicles, solar energy, and battery storage. With innovation at its core, Tesla challenges traditional automotive norms and pushes boundaries in clean technology, reshaping how we think about transportation and energy.

Tesla’s electric vehicle lineup includes the Model S, Model 3, Model X, and Model Y. Each offers cutting-edge performance, safety, and autonomous driving features. The Cybertruck and Semi expand Tesla’s reach into utility and freight. These vehicles combine sleek design with eco-conscious engineering, attracting global attention and demand

Tesla’s energy products include solar panels, Solar Roof, and energy storage solutions like Powerwall and Powerpack. These innovations enable homeowners and businesses to generate, store, and manage renewable energy efficiently. Tesla’s integrated approach promotes sustainability and reduces reliance on fossil fuels
